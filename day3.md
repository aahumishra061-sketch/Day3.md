📖 What is Role-Based Prompting?

Role-Based Prompting is a prompt engineering technique where you assign a specific role, profession, or perspective to an AI model before asking a question.

Instead of asking Claude a general question, you first define who Claude should act as.

Example:

Without Role Prompt

> How do I launch a new feature?



With Role Prompt

> You are an experienced Product Manager. How do I launch a new feature?



By providing a role, Claude responds using the knowledge, priorities, and mindset of that expert.


---

🎯 Why Role-Based Prompting Matters

AI models can answer almost any question, but without context they often provide generic responses.

Role-Based Prompting gives Claude:

A clear perspective

Relevant domain knowledge

Better decision-making context

More structured answers

Industry-specific recommendations


This leads to responses that feel more like advice from a real professional rather than a general AI assistant.
